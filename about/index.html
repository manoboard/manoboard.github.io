About

Is skateboarding a universal language?

I think so, but I intend to find out for sure. I’m a recent graduate of the University of Washington’s Museology Graduate Program traveling the world for 8 months on a Bonderman Fellowship. Along the way, I plan to ride my skateboard and hope to meet other people who like to do the same. My hope is that a shared love of skateboarding will help me overcome language barriers and cultural differences, allowing me to meet people whose stories may be quite different than my own.
